DT Smoosh (AKA Doomsday Smoosh)

Ingredients

  • Celery
  • Yellow Onion
  • Black Olives
  • Radish
  • Chickpeas
  • Anchovies
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Packaged Breadcrumbs
  • Cooking Oil

Directions

  1. Dice your celery, onion, olives, and radish.
  2. Oil a pan and heat it on medium high heat.
  3. Add your diced vegetables and chickpeas to the pan and cook until soft, 10-15 minutes.
  4. Remove pan from heat.
  5. Top with desired amounts of anchovies, parmesan cheese, and breadcrumbs.
  6. Mix together into a pastey mush, otherwise known as a "smoosh".

Enjoy!
