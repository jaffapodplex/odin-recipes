Peanut Butter and Jelly Fruit Loop Truffle Roll-Ups

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter (Can be smooth or chunky)
  • Your preferred flavor of jelly
  • Flour tortilla
  • Fruit Loops
  • Black truffle oil
  • Honey

Directions

  1. Lay out a flour tortilla and apply your peanut butter and jelly.
  2. Add Fruit Loops
  3. Drizzle black truffle oil and honey on top
  4. Close tortilla by folding the sides together.

Enjoy!
