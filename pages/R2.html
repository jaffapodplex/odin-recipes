A.P.B. (Artichoke, Pine Nuts, Bacon

Ingredients

  • Artichoke
  • Better Than Bouillon stock base, flavor of choice
  • Salt
  • Bay Leaves
  • Bacon
  • Pine Nuts

Directions

  1. Bring a pot of water to boil
  2. Add Better Than Bouillon stock base, salt, and bay leaves.
  3. Add artichokes.
  4. Reduce heat to medium and simmer artichokes for 30 minutes or until tender.
  5. While artichokes are simmering, cook bacon in the oven at 400 degrees Fahrenheit for 20 minutes. Reserve the bacon fat.
  6. When artichokes are done, remove them from the pot and place them on a baking sheet.
  7. Pour reserved bacon fat over artichokes.
  8. Bake artichokes in oven at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 30 minutes.
  9. At the same time, you can toast the pine nuts on a separate baking sheet
  10. Serve together on one plate.

Enjoy!
